Questions

Things we hear a lot.

Is Masarefy free?

Yes — all core features (SMS parsing, budgets, Zakah, gold, Arabic and English) are free. A Premium tier adds advanced parsing rules, multi-currency, and priority support.

Which banks and wallets are supported?

Any bank or wallet that sends SMS notifications. We ship pre-tuned rules for CIB, NBE, Banque Misr, QNB, AAIB, ADIB, Vodafone Cash, Etisalat Cash, Instapay, and Fawry — and you can add custom rules for any other source.

Is it really bilingual, or just translated?

Fully bilingual. Right-to-left layout, Noto Kufi Arabic, Arabic numerals (٠–٩), Hijri calendar option, and Arabic-aware parsing rules. Switch language anytime — your data, formatting, and reports follow.

Where is my data stored?

On your iPhone. iCloud sync is opt-in and end-to-end encrypted. We do not run a backend that holds your transactions.

iOS only? What about Android?

iOS first. We're focused on getting one platform truly right — Siri, widgets, lock-screen, share sheet — before splitting effort. Android is on the roadmap once iOS is stable.

How accurate is the Zakah calculation?

It follows standard fiqh: 2.5% on cash, gold, investments, and receivables above the silver/gold Nisab thresholds, with debts deducted. Gold Nisab uses live Cairo gram price; silver Nisab is editable. Hawl is tracked per-asset. You can override any input — it's a calculator, not a fatwa.
